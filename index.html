About The Last Days Evangelism Ministries

The Last Days Evangelism Ministries, also known as "God's Castle," is a vibrant Christian ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and preparing believers for His second coming.

"This is The Last Days Evangelism Ministries, we are based in Moletjie Motinti, Polokwane."

Our Location

We are located in Moletjie Motinti, Polokwane, South Africa. Our ministry serves the local community and reaches out to people across the region with the message of hope and salvation.

Our Mission

To promote the gospel of Jesus Christ in the doctrine of the ministry| To minister in the gifts of the Holy Spirit| To spread the Word of God and the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To build a strong, Holy Spirit-filled, Word-based and power-filled church, preaching the Word of God to all men, bringing them to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ.

Our Services

We hold regular services including Sunday Worship (09:00 AM), Thursday Teaching and Miracle Service (10:00 AM), and Monday Prayer Meetings (06:00 PM).

Apostle Mothushi Chuene

The founder and senior pastor of the Last Days Evangelism Ministries.

Apostle Mothushi Chuene is a devoted servant of God called to preach the Gospel in these last days. He leads the ministry with passion and dedication, teaching the Word of God with clarity and anointing. He is also the teacher of the gospel of Jesus Christ, a mentor, and a life coach.

He releases prophecies directly from the heart of God with precision and accuracy. He is gifted in demonstrating the power of the Holy Spirit alongside his preaching.

For more information about our ministry, feel free to contact us or visit us during our service times.

    About Healing Hands Care

    The Healing Hands Ministry is a compassionate outreach dedicated to providing love, care, and support to orphaned and vulnerable children in our community. Founded on the biblical principle found in James 1:27, "Religion that God our Father accepts as pure and faultless is this: to look after orphans and widows in their distress," we are committed to being the hands and feet of Jesus to children who have lost their parents or come from difficult family situations.

    Our Mission Statement

    "To provide a loving, Christ-centered environment where orphaned and vulnerable children can experience God's healing love, receive quality care, and develop into godly, productive members of society."

    Our Programs & Services

    Residential Care

    Safe, loving homes for orphaned children with house parents who provide family-style care, spiritual guidance, and emotional support.

    Education Support

    School fees, uniforms, books, and tutoring for children from preschool through university level.

    Healthcare & Nutrition

    Comprehensive healthcare, balanced meals, and nutritional support.

    Counseling & Trauma Care

    Professional counseling services for children dealing with grief, trauma, and emotional challenges.

    Spiritual Development

    Daily devotions, Sunday school, Bible studies, and character-building programs.

    Recreation & Skills

    Sports, arts, music programs, and life skills training for holistic development.
