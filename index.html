About The Last Days Evangelism Ministries

The Last Days Evangelism Ministries, also known as "God's Castle," is a vibrant Christian ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and preparing believers for His second coming.

"This is The Last Days Evangelism Ministries, we are based in Moletjie Motinti, Polokwane."

Our Location

We are located in Moletjie Motinti, Polokwane, South Africa. Our ministry serves the local community and reaches out to people across the region with the message of hope and salvation.

Our Mission

To promote the gospel of Jesus Christ in the doctrine of the ministry| To minister in the gifts of the Holy Spirit| To spread the Word of God and the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To build a strong, Holy Spirit-filled, Word-based and power-filled church, preaching the Word of God to all men, bringing them to the saving knowledge of Jesus Christ.

Our Services

We hold regular services including Sunday Worship (09:00 AM), Thursday Teaching and Miracle Service (10:00 AM), and Monday Prayer Meetings (06:00 PM).

Apostle Mothushi Chuene

The founder and senior pastor of the Last Days Evangelism Ministries.

Apostle Mothushi Chuene is a devoted servant of God called to preach the Gospel in these last days. He leads the ministry with passion and dedication, teaching the Word of God with clarity and anointing. He is also the teacher of the gospel of Jesus Christ, a mentor, and a life coach.

He releases prophecies directly from the heart of God with precision and accuracy. He is gifted in demonstrating the power of the Holy Spirit alongside his preaching.

For more information about our ministry, feel free to contact us or visit us during our service times.

About Transformed Treasures Women's Ministry

Transformed Treasures is a vibrant women's ministry group within The Last Days Evangelism Ministries, dedicated to empowering women to discover their God-given purpose and transform their lives through the power of God's Word.

In these last days, we believe God is raising up a company of women who will be transformed treasures - vessels of honor, refined by the Word of God, and prepared for the Master's use. Our ministry provides a safe, nurturing environment where women from all walks of life can grow spiritually, emotionally, and relationally.

As a group of women committed to God's Kingdom, we study the Bible together, pray for one another, and support each other through life's challenges. We believe that when women are transformed by God's Word, they become powerful agents of change in their families, communities, and the world at large.

Our name "Transformed Treasures" comes from 2 Corinthians 4:7: "But we have this treasure in jars of clay to show that this all-surpassing power is from God and not from us." We are clay vessels being transformed to carry God's precious treasure - the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Word-Based Transformation

We believe lasting transformation comes through deep engagement with God's Word. We study Scripture to renew our minds and transform our lives.

Prayer & Intercession

We are women who pray - for our families, our church, our community, and the nations. Prayer is our foundation and power source.

Sisterhood & Support

We build authentic relationships where women can be real, find encouragement, and support one another through life's journey.

Service & Outreach

Transformed women transform others. We serve our community and reach out to other women with God's love and truth.
